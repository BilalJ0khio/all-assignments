Why we need to boycott Israeli products

The boycott of Israeli products in response to the Palestine genocide situation is a moral stance against injustice. It symbolizes solidarity with the Palestinian people, who endure oppression, displacement, and violence. By boycotting Israeli goods, individuals and organizations globally aim to pressure Israel to end its occupation and human rights violations in Palestine. Economic sanctions serve as a non-violent means to advocate for justice, demanding accountability from the international community. Such actions highlight the power of collective activism and aim to promote peace, equality, and respect for human rights in the region, fostering hope for a just resolution to the conflict.

The War in Gaza

Israel has killed over 21,000 Palestinians since October 7, 2023. This includes a huge number of children and women. Most of the people killed in this war are civilians, including journalists. Israel blames Hamas for its recent genocide in the Gaza Strip. However, many, including UN Secretary-General Antonio Guterres, hold that the Hamas attacks did not happen in isolation, as they were the result of decades of oppression and occupation.
